<h3>Race Moves</h3>
<b>Elf</b> Enter an important location (your call): you can ask the GM for one fact from the history of that location.
<b>Human</b> First enter a civilized settlement: someone who respects the custom of hospitality to minstrels will take you in as their guest.
<h3>Starting Moves</h3>
<b>Arcane Art</b> Weave a performance into a basic spell - choose an ally and an effect, then (roll+CHA): 10+, the ally gets the effect, 7-9, your spell still works, but you draw unwanted attention or your magic reverberates to other targets affecting them as well.<ul><li>Heal 1d8 damage</li><li>+1d4 forward to damage</li><li>Their mind is shaken clear of one enchantment</li><li>Next time someone successfully assists the target with aid, they get +2 instead of +1</li></ul>
<b>Charming & Open</b> Speak frankly with someone. Ask their player a question from the list below. They must answer it truthfully, then they may ask you a question from the list (which you must answer truthfully).<ul><li>Whom do you serve?</li><li>What do you wish I would do?</li><li>How can I get you to _____?</li><li>What are you really feeling right now?</li><li>What do you most desire?</li></ul>
<b>Bardic Lore</b> Choose an area of expertise, when you first encounter an important creature, location, or item (your call) covered by your bardic lore you can ask the GM any one question about it; the GM will answer truthfully. The GM may then ask you what tale, song, or legend you heard that information in.<ul><li>Spells and Magicks</li><li>The Dead and Undead</li><li>Grand Histories of the Known World</li><li>A Bestiary of Creatures Unusual</li><li>The Planar Spheres</li><li>Legends of Heroes Past</li><li>Gods and Their Servants</li></ul>
<b>A Port In The Storm</b> Return to a civilized settlement you’ve visited before: Tell the GM when you were last here. They’ll tell you how it’s changed since then.
<h3>Advanced Moves 2-10</h3>
<b>Healing Song</b> Heal with Arcane Art: you heal +1d8 damage.
<b>Vicious Cacophony</b> Grant bonus damage with Arcane Art: you grant an extra +1d4 damage.
<b>It Goes To Eleven</b> Unleash a crazed performance (roll+CHA): 10+ the target attacks their nearest ally in range. 7-9 they attack their nearest ally, but you also draw their attention and ire.
<b>Metal Hurlant</b> Shout with great force or play a shattering note: choose a target (roll+CON): 10+ the target takes 1d10 damage and is deafened for a few minutes. 7-9 you still damage your target, but it’s out of control, the GM will choose an additional target nearby.
<b>A Little Help From My Friends</b> Successfully aid someone: you take +1 forward as well.
<b>Eldritch Tones</b> Your Arcane Art is strong, allowing you to choose two effects instead of one.
<b>Duelist’s Parry</b> Hack & Slash: you take +1 armor forward.
<b>Bamboozle</b> Parley with someone: 7+ you also take +1 forward with them.
<b>Multiclass Dabbler</b> Get one move from another class. Treat your level as one lower for choosing the move.
<b>Multiclass Initiate</b> Get one move from another class. Treat your level as one lower for choosing the move.
<h3>Advanced Moves 6-10</h3>
<b>Healing Chorus</b> <i>Replaces: Healing Song</i> Heal with Arcane Art: you heal +2d8 damage.
<b>Vicious Blast</b> <i>Replaces: Vicious Cacophony</i> Grant bonus damage with Arcane Art: you grant an extra +2d4 damage.
<b>Unforgettable Face</b> Meet someone you’ve met before (your call) after some time apart you take +1 forward against them.
<b>Reputation</b> First meet someone who’s heard songs about you (roll+CHA): 10+, tell the GM two things they’ve heard about you. 7-9, tell the GM one thing they’ve heard, and the GM tells you one thing.
<b>Eldritch Chord</b> <i>Replaces: Eldritch Tones</i> Arcane Art: you choose two effects. You also get to choose one of those effects to double.
<b>An Ear For Magic</b> Hear an enemy cast a spell: the GM will tell you the name of the spell and its effects. Take +1 forward when acting on the answers.
<b>Devious</b> Charming & Open: you may also ask “How are you vulnerable to me?” Your subject may not ask this question of you.
<b>Duelist’s Block</b> <i>Replaces: Duelist’s Parry</i> Hack & Slash: you take +2 armor forward.
<b>Con</b> <i>Replaces: Bamboozle</i> Parley with someone: 7+ you also take +1 forward with them and get to ask their player one question which they must answer truthfully.
<b>Multiclass Master</b> Get a move from another class. Treat your level as one lower for choosing the move.
